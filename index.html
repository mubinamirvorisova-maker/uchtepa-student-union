Who We Are

About the Student Union

🏫

School Image

Est. 2024

The Thompson International School Student Union at Uchtepa Branch is the official representative body of student voice, creativity, and leadership.

We bridge the gap between students and administration, organise cultural and academic events, and create an environment where every student can thrive, lead, and make a lasting impact.

Our mission is rooted in three pillars: Leadership — developing the next generation of changemakers; Influence — shaping school culture and policy; Action — delivering real, tangible results for our community.
